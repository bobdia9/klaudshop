ABOUT US

Welcome to Klaud Luxury, where we believe in spreading joy, one basket at a time. Our mission is simple: to provide thoughtful, beautifully crafted gift baskets that bring happiness to those who receive them.
At Klaud Luxury, we understand the power of a well-chosen gift. That's why we carefully curate each basket to ensure it's filled with only the finest treats and treasures. From sweet treats to soothing comforts, every item is selected to evoke a sense of delight and wonder.
Our slogan, "Experiencing love in a single basket", reflects our commitment to providing a truly special experience for our customers. Whether you are celebrating a milestone, showing appreciation for a loved one, or simply brightening someone's day, we have a basket to suit every occasion.
By choosing Klaud Luxury, you are not just sending a gift - you are sharing joy, comfort, and love. So why settle for an ordinary gift when you can give the gift of joy, carefully crafted and delivered in a beautiful basket?
Thank you for visiting us, and we look forward to helping you spread joy, one basket at a time!

OUR ORIGIN

The Klaud Luxury Gift Shop is a business that was founded from the deep love for creating thoughtful, beautifully curated hampers. Believing in the power of giving and making every gift special,inspiring the start of this business. Each hamper is crafted with care, blending creativity and quality to bring joy to every recipient. Whether you are sharing love or expressing gratitude, my hampers are designed to make your moments unforgettable. Dedicated to delivering excellence and helping to share meaningful expriences through personalized gifting.

THE FOUNDER
Omoigberia Olohi Precious

Omoigberia Olohi Precious, is a creative and driven entrepreneur with a passion for turning simple ideas into memorable expriences. Born and raised in Nigeria, she always believed in the importance of thoughtfulness and connection, which reflects in everthing she does.
She started the hamper business as a way to combine her love for creativity and her desire to help people celebrate life's special moments. Outside of business, she enjoys exploring new ideas, staying inspired, and continually learning to grow both personally and professionally.
